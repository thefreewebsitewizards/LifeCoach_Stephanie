About Stephanie

"Who I Am and What I Do"

Trusted Life and Relationship Coach

My name is Stephanie and I am a successful life & relationship coach as well as author of the new self-help book, titled: Dating With Purpose: How To Find The One In One Year. For as long as I can remember, I have been the sounding board for many friends and someone that strangers would vent to. I found my calling through helping these friends and strangers to feel heard and understood.

Are you having issues in your relationship or having trouble figuring out why you're not in one? Do you feel lost, lonely or empty and wish you had someone to talk to who will listen objectively? Are you or have you been in an abusive relationship that has left you stuck or scared to move forward? I offer unique talk and text therapy for men and women of all ages and a wide range of issues. You can feel free to vent, open up, cry, and ask for advice or inspiration.

You can rest assured you won't be judged, brushed off or have drugs pushed on you. I am here to bring you back to your center so you can get to the core of who you are and the root of why you have become sidetracked.

I'm here to help you look deeper and evolve into your best self through introspection, discipline and action. Even if you are already happy, there's no better time than when you're fully open and ready to meet your highest potential in life. To set up an appointment, contact me on the form below with your full name, phone number, email and a brief description of what you'd like to talk about and we can go from there. Choose love over fear and take that leap of faith with me in your corner!
